# Tarco Aviation Booking

## Product Purpose

A premium flight booking web app for Tarco Aviation — a Sudanese airline connecting Sudan to the Middle East and Africa. The app covers the full booking funnel: search, flexible-date pricing matrix, fare selection, extra services, seat map, and booking confirmation.

## Register

product

## Users

**Primary**: Sudanese travelers and diaspora booking flights from Khartoum (KRT) to destinations like Dubai, Riyadh, Jeddah, Cairo, Doha, Addis Ababa. Age 25-55. Mix of Arabic and English speakers. Booking on desktop and mobile.

**Secondary**: Business travelers flying Business/First class who expect premium service cues.

## Brand

- **Name**: Tarco Aviation
- **Tagline**: سلام وابتسام (Peace and Smile)
- **Slogan**: أسطورة أفريقيا (Legend of Africa)
- **Colors**: Tarco Red `#E31E24`, Tarco Blue `#1D4296`, Tarco Navy `#041438`
- **Tone**: Professional, warm Sudanese hospitality, trustworthy, modern
- **Typography**: Inter (English), Cairo (Arabic)
- **Languages**: Arabic (RTL primary) + English (LTR)

## Anti-References

- No gold/yellow accents (removed from previous design iteration)
- Not a generic SaaS dashboard feel
- Not clinical or cold — should feel warm and trustworthy
- Not over-decorated like budget carriers

## Strategic Principles

1. Trust at every step — users are committing money to flights
2. Clarity over cleverness — the booking funnel must be obvious
3. Bilingual parity — Arabic and English experiences must be equally good
4. Mobile-first — many users book on phone
5. Premium feel without being intimidating
